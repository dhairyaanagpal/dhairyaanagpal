# Hi, I'm Dhairya Nagpal 👋
 
## About
 
I build products, design studios, and policy frameworks, usually at the same time. I completed my undergrad from [Delhi Technological University](https://dtu.ac.in) with a Gold Medal (Rank 1) in Human-Computer Interaction and a Foundation in Data Science & Programming from [IIT Madras](https://www.iitm.ac.in/). I work across product management, strategy, policy, UX and AI frameworks and I have explored all of them extensively to the conclude that I equally love all of them.
 
I currently lead Product & UX at [Damco Group](https://www.damcogroup.com/), where I built a design and product studio from scratch (15+ designers, 30+ engagements, cross domain & industry, enterprise AI). Before this, I was at [Zomato](https://www.zomato.com/) owning the logistics cost product charter, rider incentives, payouts, cost-per-order, gigs. I've also shipped at [Housing.com](https://housing.com), [MPL](https://www.mpl.live/), [Hike](https://hike.in/), and done research at [IIT Delhi](https://home.iitd.ac.in/), [Concordia University](https://www.concordia.ca/), [ISB](https://www.isb.edu/), [SPJIMR](https://www.spjimr.org/), and [UPES](https://www.upes.ac.in/). I'm also a [United Nations Foundation Fellow](https://unfoundation.org/) working on responsible tech and AI governance, and a published researcher (ICoRD 2025, Pan IIM, UX Planet).
 
Besides tech and product, I'm an artist and sculptor. I conduct workshops in various art mediums (foam clay, resin, fluid art), and have partnered with folks like [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/) for sessions. I sell original work globally through [DNS](https://art.dhairyanagpal.com). I love playing musical instruments, dancing, and reading. At any given point in time, you'll probably find me cooking something. I have a deep interest in AI ethics, the epistemic, the ethical, the experiential, all of it.
